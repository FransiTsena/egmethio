Urban Expo

The African Urban Forum Exhibition is a premier venue for Member States, cities, partners, and stakeholders to share knowledge, build capacity, network, and showcase cutting-edge technologies, successful urban projects, and best practices from various sectors to foster collaboration and exchange to promote sustainable urbanization.

This year's African Urban Forum will focus on "Sustainable Urbanisation for Africa's Transformation: Agenda 2063," emphasizing the continent's long-term development goals to foster inclusive growth, sustainable cities, and improved quality of life for all citizens. With over 20,000 participants expected to attend the Ethio Green Mobility 2024 forum which will take place from September 4 to September 6, 2024.
