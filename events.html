Ethio Green Mobility 2024

Ethio Green Mobility 2024 will be held from November 22-30. This expo will showcase the latest innovations in sustainable mobility, including cutting-edge electric vehicles and renewable energy technologies.

Attendees will have the opportunity to engage with industry leaders through keynote speeches, interactive workshops, and panel discussions. The event also provides a platform for networking and collaboration among stakeholders from various sectors, all working to drive Ethiopia's vision for a greener future.

This expo is not only a showcase of technological advancements but also a celebration of Ethiopia's growing role in the global transition towards sustainable transport.

Objective of Events

Showcase Innovations: Highlight the latest advancements in electric vehicles (EVs) and green technologies, demonstrating cutting-edge solutions and products that contribute to sustainability and environmental protection.

Promote Sustainable Development: Foster the adoption of sustainable practices and technologies across various sectors, including transportation, energy, waste management, and agriculture, to support Ethiopia's transition to a greener economy.

Encourage Collaboration: Facilitate networking and partnerships between industry leaders, technology providers, policymakers, and environmental advocates to drive collaborative efforts and shared solutions for pressing environmental challenges.

Educate and Inspire: Provide a platform for knowledge exchange and thought leadership on best practices, emerging trends, and innovative approaches in the fields of electric mobility and green technologies.

Support Policy Development: Inform and influence policy discussions and decisions by showcasing successful implementations of green technologies and EVs, thereby supporting the development of effective policies and regulations.

Stimulate Economic Growth: Promote investment opportunities and stimulate economic growth by attracting local and international businesses, investors, and entrepreneurs interested in the green technology sector.

Raise Public Awareness: Engage the general public and increase awareness about the importance of sustainability, the benefits of green technologies, and the role of electric vehicles in reducing environmental impact.
